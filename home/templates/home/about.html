About

Fitness Madness was found by siblings Edward S. Thompsan and Elena C. Thompsan in the year 1980.

Fitness Madness offer people the chance to improve their personal health and wellbeing by offering a place for them to exercise. Each gym is equipped with the latest technology and equipment. We offer one-on-one personal training and personalised advice suited to each individual. This would include a personalised training programme, menu and training.

Customers can apply online or instore at any of our six branches.

Methods on how to improve your personal health and wellbeing

Eat healthy

Exercise regularly

Drink water

Less time on screens

Spend more time outside

Spend time with other people

Sleep well

Have fun
